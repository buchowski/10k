Forbidden wood

Five rings encircle a mystical tree. Each ring has numbered gemstones capable of unlocking the tree's secrets.

To summon the tree's knowledge you must select two gemstones per ring, in the correct sequence. While you may leverage a brute-force attack, selecting random gemstone after random gemstone, you should first be warned: There are swamp hounds in these woods!

To aid in your quest, a local Waligorian sage has provided you a set of ancient mind tricks. Leverage each mind trick wisely to select the two correct gemstones per ring. Do so in the correct sequence and you'll unlock the secrets of the mystical tree of forbidden wood!

Mind tricks (per ring)

  • Ring 1 (outermost)

    This number of horsemen, from the corners of the earth, is just as deadly in the desert heat as they are when the temperature is freezing

  • Ring 2

    This number of commands, carved out of stone in more ancient times, has since been written to papyrus, using ALL of the letters in the alphabet of the very language used here in the Forbidden Wood.

  • Ring 3

    As the ancient story goes, the world was created in this short span of days, but since then each human has been made of toad stool, swamp broth and this number of chromosomiums from two human parents.

  • Ring 4

    We've relied on this number of celestial bodies to determine time since before history, but most still don't know of the secret Walgorian month, the first month not yet aptured by calendar.

  • Ring 5 (innermost)

    In the ancient desert, a number of wisemen witnessed an event that birthed the formation of this most famous set of disciples.
